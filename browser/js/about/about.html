SpellBook

SpellBook is an interactive story book that teaches children basic programmatic logic, using fun story-lines and a unique drag-and-drop interface.

This project was created by the Enigmasters of Fullstack Academy: Austin Shoecraft, Beckylee Dell, Chandra Galbiati, and Emily Sanford. We'd like to give special credit to Griffin and Ben for all their support, assistance, and puns.
